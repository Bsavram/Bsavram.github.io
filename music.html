Why to love music...

Music makes the world go round. It gives all kind of emotions and is #relatable usualy. Listen when your bored, during class, during a jog, during a carride, really anytime. And dont forget during public transportation. But, music lets you just be in tune with yourself in the sense that, when having headphones or listening alone you feel at ease and you can go to your peacefull place without the distractions of the world around.

I listen to anything really, depending on who im with, where I am or what im in the mood for. But theres a ton of things to choose from ranging from pop, hip-hop, trap, electronic, and so on. Even other languages because you dont allows have to understand a song to get the emotions from it.
